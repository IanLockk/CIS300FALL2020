Recruitment

The Alpha Sigma Chapter of the Alpha Epsilon Pi fraternity could not be more excited for rush this semester as we look to expand our brotherhood. The relationships and bonds made through the experiences had during rush week will last a life time. For more information about recruitment at Arizona State please vist the Interfraternity Council's official website: https://eoss.asu.edu/fsl/join/fraternity

A Message from our Rush Chair

My name is Frank Levine and I am AEPi Alpha Sigma’s rush chair for 2021. The rush process is such an amazing experience where you will find the home that is made for you and I am so excited to meet the future members of Arizona State's fraternal and jewish community. Every chapter has so much to offer and will positively shape you into a confident and inspiring men. We strive each day to seek our national vision to “provide education, resources and training to the future leaders of the world’s Jewish communities.”

I myself have learned my true character and built a confidence that I truly hope to promote through each one of my brothers. I am so excited to meet the future class and bring in more brothers to such an amazing community and I look forward to seeing each member's next step forward.

Sincerely,
Frank Levine
