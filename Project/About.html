Our Chapter

Alpha Epsilon Pi provides education, resources and training to the future leaders of the world's Jewish communities.

Alpha Epsilon Pi is committed to supporting Jewish causes to reinforce these ideals through our Repair the World Fund. The Repair the World Fund works with organizations such as BBYO, Gift of Life, and many more. The Alpha Sigma chapter of AEPi was awarded a Top 10 chapter in fundraising by AEPi Nationals.

To celebrate our hard work, we enjoy socials and brotherhood events that allow us to reflect on the differences we have made in our community.
